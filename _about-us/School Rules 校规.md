<h3>School Rules</h3>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Pupils who are Singapore citizens must sing the National Anthem and take
the pledge (with the right fist placed over the heart).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Pupils are to wear the prescribed school uniform to school (including
Saturdays and school holidays, school functions and school outings).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>No modification to the uniform is allowed. Pupils must wear PE attire
during PE lessons.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>By adhering to the dress code laid down by the school, the pupil will
project an image of a disciplined and responsible self.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>Show respect and concern for self, school property and others.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>A Poi Ching School pupil is expected to behave in a manner becoming of
a responsible, polite and considerate pupil. He/She will look for the good
in others and value the differences.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintain good conduct in the classroom and during all school-related activities.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Disciplining oneself can maximize learning during curriculum time and
minimize disruption to the class/assembly.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<h3>School Hours</h3>
<p><strong>7.30 am – 1.30 pm</strong>
</p>
<h3>School Attire</h3>
<p><strong>School Uniform</strong>
</p>
<p>All Poi Ching School pupils are to wear the prescribed school uniform
whenever they enter the school premises for all activities. No modification
to the school uniform is allowed.</p>

<h3>Acceptable Hair Styles</h3>
<p><strong>Girls with Long Hair</strong>
</p>
<p>Hair must be tied neatly in ponytail or plait. Fringe must be pinned up
or cut short (above the eyebrows).</p>
