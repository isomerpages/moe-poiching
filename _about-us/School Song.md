山明水秀景物齐新  
星光照南溟  
德智体美勤修苦辛  
造就诸奇英  
时不我与同学猛进  
大家趁良辰  
培我青年学问德行  
优胜劣败要警醒

O how lovely is our country  
So peaceful and clean  
Let us build a happy nation  
Through hard work and discipline  
May knowledge and virtue guide us  
Towards excellence  
We shall overcome and triumph  
Onward forward Pei Qing School
