<h3>About Us</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:50%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/friends1.gif">
</div>
<p>The Poi Ching School Alumni Association was formed on 16th June 1998 with
the following objectives:</p>
<p>To foster closer relationship and interaction among former pupils and
staff of Poi Ching School through social and recreational activities.</p>
<p>To promote a true spirit of loyalty and love for the alma mater so as
to support the school’s academic and extra. – curricular activities</p>
<p>To develop and promote fellowship amongst former pupils of the school.</p>
<h3>History</h3>
<p>In early 1998, the head of Poi Ching School English Department, Ms Tan
Lee Cheng who is also a former student of the school, met up with a group
of former students to form a pro-temp committee.</p>
<p>The Poi Ching School Alumni Association was formed on 16th June 1998.</p>
