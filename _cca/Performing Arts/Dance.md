<p>In Poi Ching Dance CCA, we explore a variety of dance genres – from contemporary
to street dance, to the foundation of our CCA, Chinese Dance.</p>
<p>Through dance, we look into ourselves and explore ways to express our
thoughts and beliefs.</p>
