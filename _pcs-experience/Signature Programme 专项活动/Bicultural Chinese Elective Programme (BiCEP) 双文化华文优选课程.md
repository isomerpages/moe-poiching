<p>The Singapore Hokkien Huay Kuan (新加坡福建会馆) has been offering the 3-year
BiCEP (双文化特选课程) for selected students from Primary 4 to Primary 6 in all
its affiliated primary schools since the programme’s inception in 2007.
Poi Ching School has been invited to come on board to collaborate with
The Singapore Hokkien Huay Kuan to implement this BICULTURAL CHINESE ELECTIVE
PROGRAMME (BiCEP) for our selected students. This programme is designed
by a team of experienced Chinese Language teachers, in consultation with
relevant experts and it aims to develop a core group of effective bilinguals;
with a strong emphasis on empowering the participants to discover their
Asian heritage and to gain a deeper understanding of the Chinese culture
and values.</p>
<p>The programme includes the following modules:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Children’s Literature Appreciation and Creative Writing (儿童文学赏析与创作)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Chinese Culture and History (中华文化与通史)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>P4 Chinese Oratorical Skill and Broadcasting Training camp by Singapore
Hokkien Huay Kuan Arts and Cultural Troupe (福建会馆文化艺术团口语训练)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>P5 Overseas School Immersion &amp; Cultural Trip</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>P6 Graduation Ceremony</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ol>
